A blood group system used in medical and forensic biology to classify blood types based on the presence of specific molecules or antigens that are attached to red blood cells. The traditional detection methods for blood grouping are an invasive method and manual analysis, for which the results are too slow to obtain bringing incorrect diagnosis many times. In recent times AI & image processing techniques has proven to be a reliable way for automatic classification of the blood group.
